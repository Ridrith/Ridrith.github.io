About Gallowshall

Gallowshall is a dark fantasy e-zine exploring the realms of despair, intrigue, and adventure within the world of Torches in the Dark 3rd Edition. With each issue, we delve into new stories, game mechanics, and community contributions to bring the darkness to life.

Discover our past issues filled with lore, character stories, and gameplay innovations. Each issue is a treasure trove for players and game masters alike.

Dive into our collection of articles, where we explore game mechanics, storytelling tips, and player experiences. Gallowshall is your guide to mastering the dark fantasy setting.
